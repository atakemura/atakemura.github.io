      About Me

      I am currently a Ph.D. student at National Institute of Informatics (Tokyo, Japan) and Department of Informatics, The Graduate University for Advanced Studies, SOKENDAI (Tokyo, Japan) (2019-Present), working with Prof. Katsumi Inoue. I obtained my Master’s degree in Chemistry (2012) from the University of York (York, UK), where my project investigated intermolecular forces.

      I am currently a R&D Manager at INTAGE Inc., having previously held roles as a Data Scientist and Project Manager (2012-Present).
